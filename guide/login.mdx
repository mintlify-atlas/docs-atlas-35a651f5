## Overview

Once you've created your account, you can log in to access your neighborhood dashboard, view reports, and participate in community activities.

## Login Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the login page">
    Click the **"Iniciar sesión"** (Log in) button in the header, or navigate directly using the hash route:
    
    ```javascript
    // Navigate to login
    window.location.hash = '#/login';
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Fill in your login information:
    
    ```html Login Form
    <form action="" method="POST">
      <div class="form-group">
        <label for="email">Correo electrónico</label>
        <input type="email" name="email" id="email" required />
      </div>

      <div class="form-group">
        <label for="password">Contraseña</label>
        <input type="password" name="password" id="password" required/>
      </div>
    </form>
    ```
    
    **Required fields:**
    - **Email**: The email address you used during registration
    - **Password**: Your account password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete verification">
    The login form includes security verification:
    
    ```html
    <div class="h-captcha"></div>
    ```
    
    Complete the CAPTCHA challenge to verify you're not a bot.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
    After successful login, you'll be redirected to the neighborhoods page:
    
    ```html
    <a href="./pages/neighborhoods.html" class="next-step" type="submit">
      Iniciar sesión
    </a>
    ```
    
    This takes you directly to your neighborhood dashboard where you can view reports, events, and community information.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Forgot your password?">
    Currently, password recovery is handled through your neighborhood JAC administration. Contact your local JAC office for assistance resetting your password.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Login not working?">
    Common issues and solutions:
    
    - **Invalid credentials**: Double-check your email and password
    - **CAPTCHA not loading**: Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
    - **Account not found**: Verify you completed the registration process
    - **Browser compatibility**: Use a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Security verification failing?">
    If the CAPTCHA verification is not working:
    
    - Try refreshing the page
    - Clear your browser cache
    - Disable browser extensions that might interfere with CAPTCHA
    - Try a different browser or device
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security Tips

<Note>
  Keep your login credentials secure:
  - Never share your password with anyone
  - Use a strong, unique password
  - Log out when using shared devices
  - Be cautious of phishing attempts
</Note>
