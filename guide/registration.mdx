This guide walks you through creating your UniJAC account, from selecting your locality and neighborhood to setting up your credentials.

## Overview

UniJAC uses a two-step registration process to ensure you're connected to the right neighborhood community in Bogotá. The registration form collects your location information first, then your account credentials.

## Before You Begin

To complete registration, you'll need:

- The name of your **Localidad** (locality) in Bogotá
- The name of your **Barrio** (neighborhood)
- A valid email address
- Your first and last name

## Registration Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Registration">
    From the UniJAC homepage, click the **"Crear cuenta"** (Create account) button or navigate to the registration page.
    
    You'll see the form header showing **"Paso 1 de 2"** (Step 1 of 2), indicating this is a multi-step process.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Your Localidad">
    In the first dropdown field labeled **"Localidad"**, select your locality from the list.
    
    <Info>
      The localities are loaded dynamically from Bogotá's official cadastral database. If the dropdown shows "Cargando localidades..." (Loading localities...), wait a moment for the data to load.
    </Info>
    
    The dropdown contains all 20 localities of Bogotá, sorted alphabetically. Click to open the dropdown and select your locality.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Your Barrio">
    Once you select a locality, the **"Barrio"** (Neighborhood) dropdown becomes enabled.
    
    This dropdown is initially disabled (grayed out) and will only activate after you select a locality. The neighborhoods shown are filtered based on your selected locality.
    
    <Note>
      If you see "Cargando barrios..." (Loading neighborhoods...), the system is fetching the neighborhoods for your locality. This usually takes just a moment.
    </Note>
    
    Select your neighborhood from the filtered list. Neighborhoods are also sorted alphabetically for easy navigation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Name">
    Fill in the following required fields:
    
    - **Nombre** (First name): Enter your first name
    - **Apellido** (Last name): Enter your last name
    
    Both fields are required and marked with the `required` attribute.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Accept Terms and Continue">
    Before continuing, note the terms statement:
    
    > "Al continuar, aceptas el uso de cookies y la política de privacidad al proyecto UniJAC para crear una cuenta."
    > 
    > (By continuing, you accept the use of cookies and the privacy policy of the UniJAC project to create an account.)
    
    Click the **"Continuar"** (Continue) button to proceed to step 2.
    
    <Tip>
      If you already have an account, you can click **"Iniciar sesión"** (Log in) below the Continue button to go to the login page instead.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Email and Password">
    The form header now shows **"Paso 2 de 2"** (Step 2 of 2).
    
    Fill in your account credentials:
    
    - **Correo electrónico** (Email): Enter a valid email address
    - **Contraseña** (Password): Create a secure password
    
    Both fields are required. The email field uses HTML5 email validation to ensure proper format.
    
    <Warning>
      Choose a strong password to protect your account. UniJAC stores community reports and personal information.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Account">
    Click the **"Crear cuenta"** (Create account) button to complete registration.
    
    Upon successful registration, you'll be redirected to the dashboard summary page where you can start exploring your neighborhood's activity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Field Validation

All form fields include validation:

| Field | Validation | Notes |
|-------|------------|-------|
| Localidad | Required, must select from dropdown | Populated from Bogotá cadastral API |
| Barrio | Required, must select from dropdown | Filtered based on selected Localidad |
| Nombre | Required text field | Your first name |
| Apellido | Required text field | Your last name |
| Correo electrónico | Required, valid email format | Used for login and notifications |
| Contraseña | Required text field | Stored securely |

## Navigating Between Steps

The registration form includes navigation controls:

- **Back button**: In step 1, a back arrow button appears in the header to return to the previous page
- **Continuar button**: Advances from step 1 to step 2
- **Step indicator**: Shows current progress ("Paso 1 de 2" or "Paso 2 de 2")

<Note>
  The form uses `fieldset` elements with `aria-label` attributes for accessibility, making it screen-reader friendly.
</Note>

## After Registration

Once you complete registration:

1. **Automatic login**: You're automatically logged into your new account
2. **Dashboard redirect**: You're taken to `/pages/dashboard/summary.html`
3. **Neighborhood selection**: On first visit, a welcome modal prompts you to confirm or search for your neighborhood
4. **Ready to participate**: You can now view reports, RSVP to events, and submit your own reports

## Troubleshooting

### "Error cargando localidades" appears

This means the API request to fetch localities failed. Check your internet connection and refresh the page.

### "Error cargando barrios" appears

The neighborhoods API failed to load. Try selecting a different locality or refresh the page.

### Dropdown fields are disabled

The **Barrio** dropdown remains disabled until you select a **Localidad**. This is intentional behavior.

### Can't click Continue button

Ensure all required fields in Step 1 are filled:
- Localidad selected
- Barrio selected  
- Nombre filled
- Apellido filled

## Data Sources

UniJAC integrates with official Bogotá government services:

- **Localities API**: `serviciosgis.catastrobogota.gov.co` - Provides the list of all Bogotá localities
- **Neighborhoods API**: Same service, filtered by locality code - Provides neighborhoods within each locality

These APIs ensure your neighborhood data is accurate and up-to-date with official records.

## Privacy and Data Usage

By creating an account, you agree to:

- Cookie usage for session management
- Storage of your name, email, and neighborhood information
- Association of your reports with your account (unless submitted anonymously)

For full details, refer to UniJAC's privacy policy.
