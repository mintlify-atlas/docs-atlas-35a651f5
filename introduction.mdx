## What is UniJAC?

UniJAC is a comprehensive web-based community management platform designed to strengthen communication and participation among residents of Bogotá neighborhoods. Built to support sustainable urban development (UN SDG 11), UniJAC connects residents with their local Junta de Acción Comunal (JAC) to report issues, discover events, and stay informed about neighborhood activities.

The platform serves as a centralized digital hub where community members can:

- Report infrastructure problems, safety concerns, and public space issues
- Stay informed about neighborhood events and activities
- View important schedules like garbage collection times
- Track community reports and their resolution status
- Connect with other residents and JAC members

## Who is UniJAC for?

### For residents

UniJAC empowers you to take an active role in your community. Whether you need to report a broken streetlight, find out about upcoming events, or stay connected with your neighbors, UniJAC provides a simple, accessible way to engage with your neighborhood.

### For JAC members

As a JAC member, UniJAC gives you the tools to manage community reports, organize events, and communicate effectively with residents. The platform helps you track issues, coordinate responses, and keep your neighborhood informed.

## Technology and accessibility

UniJAC is built as a modern single-page application using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. The platform features:

- **Hash-based routing** for seamless navigation without page reloads
- **Responsive design** that works on desktop and mobile devices
- **Accessibility features** including font size adjustment, high contrast mode, and ARIA labels
- **No installation required** - access directly through your web browser
