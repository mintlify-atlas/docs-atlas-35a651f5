## What is JAC?

The **Junta de Acción Comunal (JAC)** is a civic organization that represents and manages the interests of neighborhood communities in Bogotá. JAC members are elected community leaders responsible for coordinating activities, addressing local issues, and fostering communication between residents and local authorities.

UniJAC provides JAC members with digital tools to efficiently manage their neighborhoods and strengthen community participation.

## JAC Role in UniJAC

As a JAC member, you have special administrative privileges that enable you to:

- **Review and manage** community reports about infrastructure, security, and public services
- **Organize and publish** community events and activities
- **Communicate** with residents through announcements and news
- **Maintain** neighborhood schedules (garbage collection, maintenance, etc.)
- **Monitor** community engagement and participation metrics

## Accessing the Admin Panel

JAC members access the administrative dashboard through their UniJAC account with elevated permissions:

1. Log in to your UniJAC account at the main page
2. Navigate to your registered neighborhood
3. The admin panel will be available with additional management features
4. Use the navigation menu to access different administrative sections

<Note>
Only verified JAC members have access to administrative features. Contact UniJAC support if you need admin access verification.
</Note>

## Admin Dashboard Features

The JAC administration dashboard provides several key sections:

### Report Management
Review, prioritize, and update the status of community reports including:
- Daños (infrastructure damage)
- Extravíos (lost items/pets)
- Puntos críticos (critical security areas)
- Servicios públicos (public service issues)
- Espacio público (public space concerns)

### Event Organization
Create and manage community events such as:
- Rumba terapia (community dance therapy)
- Vaccination drives
- Sports activities
- Cultural celebrations
- Community meetings

### Community Communication
Publish announcements and news to keep residents informed about:
- Important neighborhood updates
- Schedule changes
- Safety alerts
- Community initiatives

### Schedule Management
Maintain important neighborhood schedules like:
- Garbage collection days and times (Lun, Mie, Vie – 7:00 PM)
- Maintenance schedules
- Service provider visits

## JAC Responsibilities

As a JAC administrator, your key responsibilities include:

### Timely Response
Review and respond to community reports within a reasonable timeframe to maintain trust and engagement.

### Accurate Information
Ensure all published information (events, schedules, announcements) is accurate and up-to-date.

### Community Engagement
Foster active participation by encouraging residents to report issues and attend events.

### Coordination
Work with local authorities and service providers to resolve community issues effectively.

### Transparency
Maintain open communication about the status of reports and ongoing community initiatives.

## Dashboard Metrics

The admin panel displays summary statistics to help you monitor community activity:

- **Eventos**: Number of upcoming community events
- **Daños**: Active infrastructure damage reports
- **Extravíos**: Lost and found reports
- **Aseo**: Public sanitation and cleanliness reports

These metrics help you prioritize your administrative tasks and identify areas needing attention.
